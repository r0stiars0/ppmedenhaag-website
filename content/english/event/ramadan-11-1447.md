To help ensure the event runs smoothly, we kindly request the following:

1. Please follow the instructions of the Ramadan Committee team wearing uniforms.
2. Attendees are expected to arrive on time at **17:00** to join the lectures.
3. For the **Al Ittihaad** group (Indonesian language), the Islamic lecture will be given by **Ustadz Muhammad Iqbal** in the **large lower room** for both brothers and sisters.
4. Before iftar, brothers from the lower floor are requested to move upstairs via the large front staircase. Youth and adult sisters should head downstairs via the small back staircase. After Maghrib, the lectures will continue, and the original room composition will resume.
5. Please take your place for iftar as directed by the committee. Keep the saf (rows) tight.
6. Takjil (snacks) will be distributed to everyone. The warm meal will be served after Maghrib.
7. Please maintain cleanliness and do not waste food or drinks. We encourage you to bring your own water bottle from home.


Let us maintain adab (etiquette) and a warm sense of community with smiles and respect, so that our worship is fully accepted by Him. Aamiin.
