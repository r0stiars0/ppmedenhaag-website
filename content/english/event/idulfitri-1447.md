Assalamu 'alaykum wa rahmatullahi wa barakaatuh.

Bismillahirrahmanirrahim,

In cooperation with Stichting Masdjid Al Hikmah, Al Hikmah shares the following announcements.

Alhamdulillah, after a blessed month of fasting, prayer, and reflection, we are approaching the joyous occasion of Eid Al-Fitr 1447H — a celebration of gratitude, renewal, and togetherness for the Muslim community.

Eid Al-Fitr marks the end of Ramadan, the holy month in which Muslims around the world fast from dawn to sunset, engage in extra worship, and strive to draw closer to Allah. It is a day of immense joy and thankfulness — a reward from Allah for the patience and devotion shown throughout the month.

The Eid prayer is an important communal act of worship. It is an occasion that brings the entire Muslim community together — men, women, and children — with sholat, listen to the khutbah (sermon), and celebrate the blessings He has bestowed upon us.

We warmly invite all brothers and sisters to join us for the Eid Al-Fitr prayer at Masjid Al Hikmah in Den Haag. Let us begin this blessed day together in unity, gratitude, and joy.
