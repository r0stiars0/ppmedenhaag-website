Assalamu'alaykum wa rahmatullahi wa barakaatuh,

PPME Den Haag, in collaboration with Dompet Dhuafa, invites us all to support our brothers and sisters in Sumatra who are still in need through the Ramadan aid package **Box of Happiness**.

> *"The example of those who spend their wealth in the way of Allah is like a grain of corn that sprouts seven ears, and in every ear there are a hundred grains. Allah multiplies for whom He wills."*
> **(QS. Al-Baqarah: 261)**

Let us make this Ramadan more meaningful by sharing happiness. No matter how big or small our contribution — it can mean the world to those who receive it.
