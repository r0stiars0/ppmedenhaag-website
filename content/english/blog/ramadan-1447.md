Bismillaahirrohmanirrohiim.

Assalamu'alaykum wa rahmatullahi wa barakaatuh, dear youth and community members of PPME Den Haag,

The blessed month of Ramadan 1447 is a time for reflection, spiritual growth, and connection. To inspire and strengthen our youth in their faith, we proudly present the Ramadan Youth Program at Masjid Al Hikmah. Over four Saturdays, we will dive deeper into Islam together with various speakers, covering topics ranging from the meaning of the Shahada to practicing your faith in daily life. Whether you come for the knowledge, the atmosphere, or the communal Iftar, you are most welcome to participate in these educational sessions.
